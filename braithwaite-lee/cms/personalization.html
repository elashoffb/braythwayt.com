CMS' mail merge module includes personalizations. Personalized letters include specific passages which do not appear for all recipients.

In this example, a personalization, the "postscriptum" is configured where Mr. Lee's letter will include a short note about golf while the remining recipients will have no note at all.

Personalizations extend to emails and faxes as well: when merging any number of letters, CMS can personalize the message for specific recipients.
