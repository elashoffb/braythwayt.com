## Gruber nails the word "fanboy"

John Gruber [ripping into Dan Lyons](http://daringfireball.net/linked/2012/01/10/samsung-lyons):

> This is just another case where the person wielding the word fanboy is the one making the over-the-top head-in-the-sand unreasonable argument.

My experience with ad hominem labels like "fanboy" and "[JavaScript Programmer](http://raganwald.posterous.com/weak-players)" is exactly this: When someone plays the man instead of the ball, it's nearly always because the ball is beyond his intellectual reach.
