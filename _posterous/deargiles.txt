## Dear Giles:

Thank you.

To everyone else: If you already know who Giles is, you now know that I feel grateful to him. If you don't know who he is, hasten over to his [blog](http://gilesbowkett.blogspot.com/). Here are a few recent samples that I endorse:

> "Decision is the ultimate power." The only reason your e-mail is Steve Jobs' free advertizing service is because Steve Jobs (or someone in his army of minions) decided to put it there, and you never decided anything different. Changing the default is near-effortless, but it's not something many people do; that tiny space between effortless and near-effortless functions like a vast interstellar voyage. ---[What "Sent From My iPhone" Means To Me](http://gilesbowkett.blogspot.com/2010/08/what-sent-from-my-iphone-means-to-me.html)

> The winning part of "it's Facebook, but for dogs!" isn't the "Facebook" part, it's the "for dogs!" part, and really it's the "for" part, more than any other part, that brings home the bacon. It's probably better to do exactly what your customer needs, but in an incompetent way, than it is to do something which nobody needs, better than anyone would have ever thought possible.
> 
> The only problem is that we geeks fucking love doing something which nobody needs, better than anyone would have ever thought possible.---[The Sites Which Dominate Niche Markets](http://gilesbowkett.blogspot.com/2010/08/sites-which-dominate-niche-markets.html)

> Big thing I've figured out recently: if somebody wants to play a fierce, competitive game of "Who's The Biggest Asshole?" with you, it's okay to let them win---[Better Late Than Never](http://gilesbowkett.blogspot.com/2010/08/better-late-than-never.html)
